Why use On-Hour-Time?

On-Hour-Time is a web-based application intended to provide a simplified form of online scheduling and availability. Our mission is to provide users with an efficient way to invite friends/co-workers and compare availabilities. On-Hour-Time can be used by any user who is looking for a fast and efficient way to compare availabilities. It can be used by but is not limited to students, teachers, recruiters, and your every-day soccer mom.
